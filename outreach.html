Outreach

I work to inspire young people to love mathematics in the following ways:
  • I am a co-organizer of the Quantum Algebra and Topology Seminar and the Graduate Quantum Algebra and Topology Seminar at UCSB.
  • I am the President and founder of the UCSB AWM Student Chapter.
  • I co-mentored with Sanjay Kumar a project for the 2022 REU Program. For the description of the project you can click here. My mentees made a poster of our results which you can view here.
  • I am a graduate mentor and co-organizer for the Directed Reading Program at UC Santa Barbara. My student for 2020-2021 won the poster competition. You can view her poster here. My students for 2021-2022 also won the poster competition. You can view their poster here.
  • I am a mentor for Women in Stem Mentorship Program at UC Santa Barbara
  • I have been an active volunteer with a few Math Circles. At UCSB I have worked with Maria Isabel Bueno Cachadina to create a Math Circle for incoming undergraduates. While at the University of Oklahoma, I was the cofounder of the Math Teacher's Circle in Oklahoma City and while at UC Davis I was a volunteer at the Davis Math Circle.
