Quantum Algebra and Topology Seminar at UC Santa Barbara

Organizers: Aaron Bagheri and Melody Molander

The UCSB Quantum Algebra and Topology Seminar will usually meet on Tuesdays at 2 pm Pacific Time.

For Spring 2022, this seminar will be joint with the Graduate Quantum Topology & Algebra Seminar

Talks with speakers from outside of UCSB will be given virtually. If you are interested in attending virtually, please email me and I will send you the Zoom link.
